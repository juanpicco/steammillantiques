Steam Mill Antiques

"I buy and sell all kinds of old stuff"

Steam Mill Antiques is a country antique shop located in the beautiful White Mountains of Maine.

Inside our large, circa 1820 farmhouse and barn you will find a wide range of antiques, collectibles, books and other stuff.

Anything that you could find in an antique shop you may find here! Our stock is always changing so it's always worth the time to browse the shop.
